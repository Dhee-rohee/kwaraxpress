        Why We Stand Out...

        Ifesinachi Transport Company offers city-to-city bus tickets for very low prices around the country, all bookable from your computer or smartphone. Our system works very efficiently from the moment you book your tickets to the moment you arrive at your destination. Our luxury buses offer free Wi-Fi, at-seat power outlets, and a green alternative way to travel.

        Meticulously maintained with professional drivers at the wheel, when you travel with Ifesinachi, you will be riding in comfort and confidence. You can be assured of a great experience and overall satisfaction when you choose us. Our professional staff and fleet of clean, comfortable, well maintained buses allow us to provide you the services you deserve irrespective of the distance you travel.

        More Than 1000 Cars

        Our buses are numerous and neat. They come in fleets in time to replace worn out buses. This is a sure reassuring fact to any traveler, because they are sure of getting “up and running” buses at any time they want to make their travel. We have more than enough buses to serve just any number of passengers we can get any day.

        Offers for Group Booking

        We give travelers the great opportunity of booking tickets at once for plenty people. No one needs to waste their time coming one after the other to book for tickets in the case of group travelling. Just one person can book for a group without any stress!

        24/7 Customer Service

        We offer services to people travelling in the morning as well as to people travelling at night. Our services are 24/7; we are always available to receive anyone in any of our branches at any time of the day. So, come whenever you want and you will get a smiley face waiting to give you a warm welcome.

        Lowest Prices & Safety

        Our company is about the safest transport company you can get. We have the least record of mishaps and the most matured drivers. Our drivers are well trained, equipped and reliable. Our prices are very attractive owing to the fact that we are already established and strong in the business.
